Clustering coefficient


\[K_v = \text{Number of neighbours of $v$} \\ E_v = \text{Number of links between neighbours of $v$} \\ \\ \text{Clustering}(\text{network}) = \frac{1}{N} \sum_v \frac{E_v}{\frac{1}{2} K_v (K_v - 1)}\]
